Academic Projects:

Semantic Segmentation of LIDAR 3D point clouds using Deep Learning

  • Collaboration of machine learning and autonomous driving groups from Bosch and machine learning group of University of Freiburg.
  • LIDAR perception in urban environment for autonomous driving with 11-class semantic point cloud segmentation from 64-channel LiDAR sensor
  • Literature review for semantic image segmentation and point cloud classification.
  • Designing of novel deep learning architecture for end-to-end point cloud segmentation.
  • Development of Python and Matlab scripts for pre-processing of dataset and post-processing and analysis of results.
  • C++ development for customization of Caffe – deep learning library.
  • Scientific discussions, collaborations and presentation of results with academic and corporate supervisors.
  • This thesis caters Perception for 64-channel LiDAR sensor for autonomous driving urban environment.
  • Deep convolutional neural network (CNN) architecture to perform novel 11-class end-to-end semantic point cloud segmentation overcoming classical ML pipeline.
  • Qualitative, quantitative analysis of experiments and results showing promising. Scientific discussions, collaborations and presentation of results with academic and corporate supervisors
